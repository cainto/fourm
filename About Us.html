History and Beleif of Tzu Chi

Established by Master Cheng Yen in 1966

Tzu Chi is a Buddhist humanitarian organization founded by Dharma Master Cheng Yen in Taiwan. Its core principles are Compassion, Relief, Joy, and Giving, rooted in Buddhist values of selfless service and love for all beings.


Tzu Chi emphasizes "Helping with wisdom and love", meaning aid should be practical, sustainable, and given with humility, not superiority. Its volunteers practice "Great Love", serving all people regardless of religion or background.
